Our Process

At Pure Woodique, we are dedicated to creating beautiful and functional kitchenware from upcycled materials. Our mission is to combine sustainability with craftsmanship, providing unique pieces that tell a story. Each item is handmade with care, ensuring quality and individuality in every piece.

Handling Wooden Products

Wooden kitchenware is not only beautiful but also durable when properly cared for. Follow these simple steps to ensure your wooden products last a lifetime:

  • Washing: Hand wash your wooden items with mild soap and warm water. Avoid soaking them for long periods or placing them in the dishwasher, as this can cause the wood to crack and warp.
  • Drying: Immediately after washing, dry your wooden products with a clean towel. Let them air dry completely before storing them.
  • Storing: Store wooden kitchenware in a dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. This helps to prevent warping and cracking.
