Woodware Care

  • Hand wash your wooden items with mild soap and warm water. Avoid soaking them for long periods or placing them in the dishwasher, as this can cause the wood to crack and warp.

  • Immediately after washing, dry your wooden products with a clean towel. Let them air dry completely before storing them.

  • Store wooden kitchenware in a dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. This helps to prevent warping and cracking.
